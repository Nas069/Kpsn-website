KPSN Organizational Structure

Our structure is designed to ensure full participation and coordination across all levels of Kebbi State:

  • Ward Coordinators – Grassroots mobilizers, responsible for organizing members in each ward.
  • Local Government Area (LGA) Coordinators – Supervise and support all ward coordinators under their LGA.
  • Zonal Leaders – Cover 3 zones: Kebbi Central, North, and South. Coordinate activities among LGAs in each zone.
  • State Executive Council – Lead decision-making and coordinate the entire network across the state.
